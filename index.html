How To Cook?

1. Cut A Burger Bun Horizontally

2. Apply Sauce On The Lower Piece

3. Add Sallet On Sauce

4. Add A Chicken Patty

5.Add Cheese

6. Add pickles And Onion Rings

7. Cover Your Burger With The Top Of Your Bun


8. Your Burger Is Ready Serve With Drink And Fries
